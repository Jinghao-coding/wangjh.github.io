About Me

Researcher & PhD Candidate

I am a PhD candidate in Software Engineering at Beihang University (BUAA), advised by Prof. Chunming Hu. My research focuses on building efficient and intelligent systems for cloud computing and AI infrastructure.

I am particularly interested in GPU cluster scheduling, performance prediction for deep learning and LLM workloads, and resource management for microservices. My work has been recognized with the Best Paper Award at IEEE JCC 2024.
